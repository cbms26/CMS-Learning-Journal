Week 6

Learning Activities & Resources

In week 6, we mainly focused on our group assessment, which is to develop James Cook University Brisbane Student Board website. All members of our group are connected with each other on various platforms. The private Slack channel is used for team communication, the Github Repository is for managing version control and the Github Project will be used for project management.
As per the client's requirement, we have completed the Planning Phase of our project. This phase played a critical role in ensuring what needs to be done next by identifying key tasks and setting priorities. It consisted of sharing individual ideas on major pages of the project, highlighting all possible sub-menus, page contents, internal and external links to the pages. Additionaly, the rough visual design for each page has been constructed, allowing us to proceed to the Development Phase of our project.
The project website is to be developed via local environment instead of developing directly on live server as to further understand the working of recommended web development approach, i.e. Local environment, Staging process and then live production.

In summary, our project will consists of three phase, they are;

  • Planning Phase
  • Development/Design Phase
  • Production Phase
    • These phases will guide us to the success of the project.
